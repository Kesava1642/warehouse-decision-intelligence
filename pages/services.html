Emergency Services Finder (Pilot)

Find urgent support when your usual engineer is unavailable. This pilot connects UK warehouses with providers offering same-day, next-day, or this-week callouts.

When this helps

Use the Emergency Services Finder when an issue is blocking safe operation or shipping, and you need to know who might still be available.

Emergency exit / door / shutter failure

Fire exit, loading door, or shutter stuck open or closed, affecting safety or security.

Dock bay / leveller issue

Dock leveller, dock shelter, or edge-of-dock issue stopping vehicles from loading or unloading safely.

Printer / label / IT failure

Label printers, scanners, or key warehouse IT failing and threatening despatch cut-offs.

Electrical / maintenance urgent support

Urgent electrical or maintenance jobs where waiting for the usual contractor is not practical.
