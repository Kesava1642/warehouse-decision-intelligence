Incident timeline

60-minute + 24-hour checkpoints with accountable actions.

T+0–10 min
Stabilise — isolate risk, confirm safety, assign incident owner.
T+10–60 min
Continuity actions — throughput workaround, comms plan, supplier-neutral options list.
T+1–24 hr
Decision pack — costs, risks, recommendation, and next-step owners logged.

Continuity versioning

Department runbooks with RACI + change history.

  • Operations — peak volume workaround, labour redeploy, cut-off decisions
  • IT / Network — scanner outage steps, label printing fallback, escalation
  • Transport — linehaul contingency, collection cut-offs, third-party overflow
  • Customer Service — SLA comms templates, exception rules, client updates

Innovation mechanism

What makes Kalvra different (visible on-site, not only in pitch decks).

  • Decision Pack — scoring + constraints + risks + actions (print/PDF ready)
  • Audit trail — every decision has owner, timestamp, and rationale
  • Scenario runbooks — structured first-hour + first-day continuity playbooks
Note: Kalvra is not a repair contractor. Any supplier engagement is optional and contracted directly by the customer. This page demonstrates the control layer concept used to keep decisions consistent and reviewable.
