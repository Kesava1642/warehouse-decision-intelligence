Business model & viability

How this service creates value and sustains itself.

1

What is sold

This service delivers structured operational decision assessments for warehouse and logistics-related decisions.

Each engagement results in a documented decision report with rationale, risks, and next actions.

2

Who pays

Target customers include warehouse operators, logistics SMEs, and growing businesses that make recurring operational investment decisions but lack independent decision intelligence.

3

How it is delivered

Assessments are delivered through a combination of structured input collection, framework-based scoring, and human review.

Site visits may be included where required, but are not mandatory for every engagement.

4

How it scales

The framework is designed to be repeatable across use cases, enabling scale through standardised assessments, modular decision areas, and potential future digital tooling.

5

Why this is viable

By focusing on decision quality rather than procurement or consultancy, the model avoids supplier bias and supports long-term trust and repeat demand.

This page is intended for endorsing bodies and stakeholders evaluating the viability of this project. No financial projections or client names are included at this stage.
