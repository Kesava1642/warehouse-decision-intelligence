Refund Policy

Our refund and cancellation terms for paid services.

Last updated: January 2026

1. Overview

We want you to be satisfied with our services. This policy explains when and how you can request a refund or cancel an engagement.

Refund terms vary depending on the service tier and the stage of work completion.

2. Refund rules by service tier

Starter (Single-site assessment)

  • Before work begins: Full refund available if you cancel within 48 hours of payment and before we have started the assessment
  • After work has started: No refund, as the assessment process requires upfront analysis and preparation
  • After delivery: No refund, as the service has been fully delivered

Team (Multi-area or ongoing support)

  • Before work begins: Full refund available if you cancel within 7 days of payment and before any work has commenced
  • During the engagement: Pro-rata refund may be available for unused portions of ongoing support, calculated based on work completed. Minimum charge applies for work already delivered.
  • After final delivery: No refund for completed deliverables

Partner (Custom or extended engagement)

  • Before work begins: Refund terms will be specified in your individual agreement
  • During the engagement: Refund terms for early termination will be specified in your individual agreement, typically covering work completed plus reasonable wind-down costs
  • After completion: No refund for completed deliverables

Partner engagements are governed by individual agreements which may include specific refund and termination clauses.

3. Cancellation window

To be eligible for a full refund, cancellation requests must be received:

  • Starter: Within 48 hours of payment
  • Team: Within 7 days of payment
  • Partner: As specified in your agreement

Cancellation requests received after these windows, or after work has commenced, will be assessed on a case-by-case basis.

4. When work has started

Work is considered to have started when any of the following occur:

  • We have conducted a discovery call or site assessment
  • We have begun analysis of your requirements
  • We have started preparing your assessment or report
  • We have scheduled or conducted a site visit

Once work has started, refunds are generally not available as we have committed time and resources to your engagement. However, we will work with you to find a fair resolution if circumstances change significantly.

5. Partial refunds

In certain circumstances, we may offer a partial refund:

  • If we are unable to complete the agreed scope of work for reasons within our control
  • If there is a significant delay on our part that materially affects the value of the service
  • For ongoing support packages where the engagement is terminated early by mutual agreement

Partial refunds will be calculated fairly based on work completed and value delivered.

6. Non-refundable items

The following are not eligible for refund:

  • Services that have been fully delivered
  • Work completed before cancellation request
  • Third-party costs incurred on your behalf (e.g., travel expenses if agreed)
  • Customised deliverables prepared specifically for you

7. How to request a refund

To request a refund or discuss cancellation:

  1. Email us at [your-email@example.com] with the subject line "Refund Request"
  2. Include your name, company, and details of the service purchased
  3. Explain the reason for your refund request

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 5 working days.

8. Refund processing

Approved refunds will be processed:

  • Using the same payment method as the original transaction where possible
  • Within 14 days of approval
  • Minus any applicable fees or charges for work already completed

9. Disputes

If you are unhappy with our decision on a refund request, please contact us to discuss. We are committed to finding fair resolutions and will consider all reasonable requests.

If we cannot reach agreement, disputes will be handled in accordance with our Terms of Service.
