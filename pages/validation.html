Validation plan (Pilot)

What we will validate during the pilot and what evidence we will publish (anonymised).

What we will validate during the pilot

  • Demand: inbound enquiries via the Pilot access form
  • Problem fit: interviews with operations managers (planned)
  • Delivery fit: ability to produce decision summaries within agreed turnaround
  • Pricing: pilot pricing response (planned)
  • Evidence: anonymised before/after decision examples

Evidence we will publish (anonymised)

  • 3 anonymised decision summaries
  • 1 incident coordination workflow example
  • 1 delivery post-mortem (what worked / what didn’t)
