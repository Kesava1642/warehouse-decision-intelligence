# BCP Intake Form (10–12 questions)

Use this to gather inputs for building the lean BCP. Keep answers concise.

---

1. **Organisation / site name**  
   [Text]

2. **Locations in scope**  
   [List sites/addresses covered by this BCP]

3. **Departments / functions in scope**  
   [e.g. Warehouse ops, IT, HR, Comms]

4. **Top 3–5 critical activities** (must continue or recover first)  
   [List]

5. **Maximum tolerable downtime (MTD)** for each critical activity (if known)  
   [e.g. Activity A: 4 hours; Activity B: 24 hours]

6. **Recovery time objective (RTO)** target for each  
   [e.g. Activity A: 2 hours; Activity B: 12 hours]

7. **Key systems** (WMS, ERP, comms, etc.) that support critical activities  
   [List]

8. **Key suppliers / vendors** that could cause disruption if unavailable  
   [List]

9. **Who is the Incident Lead?** (role or named person)  
   [Text]

10. **Who needs to be in the governance team?** (roles: Ops, IT, People/Safety, Comms, Supplier)  
    [Names or roles]

11. **Internal communications** — who speaks to staff, and who approves external messages?  
    [Brief]

12. **Top 3 disruption scenarios** you want explicitly covered  
    [e.g. Power outage, key system down, key person unavailable]

---

*Kalvra uses these answers to populate the lean BCP template. No long narrative required.*
